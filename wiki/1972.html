Deep Space Rendez-vous in 1972[edit]

  • February 21 — Luna 20, 55g from Appollonius Crater (sample return mission)
  • April 21 — Apollo 16, 95 kg from Descartes Highland (sample return mission)
  • July 22 — Venera 8 atmospheric probe worked for 50 min on the Venerian surface
  • December 11 — Apollo 17, 111 kg from Taurus-Littrow (sample return mission)
